Windchill PTC Modules

Windchill from PTC is a Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) software that helps organizations manage product data, processes, and workflows. It consists of various modules that serve different aspects of PLM. Here are the main Windchill PTC modules:

1. Core PLM Modules

Windchill PDMLink: Centralized product data management (PDM) for CAD files, documents, and BOMs (Bill of Materials).

Windchill ProjectLink: Collaborative project management and team collaboration module.

Windchill MPMLink: Manufacturing Process Management (MPM) to connect engineering BOMs (eBOMs) with manufacturing BOMs (mBOMs).

2. Quality & Compliance

Windchill Quality Management System (QMS): Manages CAPAs, non-conformances, audits, and supplier quality.

Windchill Risk & Reliability: Failure Mode and Effects Analysis (FMEA), Fault Tree Analysis (FTA), and reliability analysis.

3. Manufacturing & Service Modules

Windchill Service Parts Management: Optimizes spare parts management.

Windchill Service Information Manager (SIM): Manages service documentation, manuals, and technical publications.

4. Advanced Capabilities & Extensions

Windchill Navigate: Role-based access to PLM data using IoT and AR integrations.

Windchill Supplier Management: Manages supplier data and approved vendor lists (AVL).

Windchill Analytics: Provides insights into product performance and operational efficiency.

Windchill Change Management: Streamlines engineering changes and approval workflows.
